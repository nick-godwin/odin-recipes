Peanut Butter and Jelly

Peanut butter and Jelly sandwhich recipe:

It is best to use the freshest possible ingedients.

And always make PBJs with love!

  1. Peanut Butter
  2. Jelly
  3. Two(2) slices of bread
  • Spread the peanut butter on the first slice of bread
  • Spread the jelly on the second slice of bread
  • Place the bread slices onto of eachother, with the peanutbutter and jelly touching
